Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a vehicle with Fastlane Transport?
Booking a vehicle is simple and convenient. You can contact us directly via phone or WhatsApp, and our team will guide you through available options, pricing, and confirmation. We also assist you in choosing the best vehicle based on your travel needs.
Do you provide airport pickup and drop-off services?
Yes, we offer reliable airport pickup and drop-off services across Uganda. Our drivers track your flight to ensure timely pickup, and we guarantee a smooth and comfortable transfer to your destination.
Are your drivers professional and experienced?
Absolutely. All our drivers are licensed, well-trained, and experienced in both city and long-distance travel. They prioritize safety, professionalism, and customer satisfaction at all times.
What documents are required to rent a vehicle?
To rent a vehicle, you will need a valid driving license and a national ID or passport. International clients can use their international driving permits along with their passport.
Can tourists and international visitors rent vehicles?
Yes, we welcome tourists and international clients. Our services are tailored to meet travel needs for visitors exploring Uganda, including guided tours and self-drive options.
Do you offer chauffeur (driver) services?
Yes, we provide professional chauffeur services. If you prefer not to drive, our drivers will handle everything while you enjoy a comfortable and stress-free journey.
Are your vehicles insured?
All our vehicles are fully insured to ensure maximum safety and protection for our clients during travel.
Do you offer long-term or monthly rentals?
Yes, we provide flexible rental plans including daily, weekly, and monthly rentals. Long-term clients benefit from discounted rates and customized packages.
Do you provide corporate transport services?
Yes, we work with companies and organizations to provide reliable corporate transport solutions, including staff transport, executive travel, and business trips.
Is roadside assistance available?
Yes, we provide 24/7 roadside assistance to ensure you are supported in case of any emergencies during your trip.
Can I cancel or modify my booking?
Yes, bookings can be modified or canceled based on our cancellation policy. We recommend informing us in advance to avoid any charges.
Do you operate across all regions in Uganda?
Yes, our services are available nationwide, covering major cities, towns, and tourist destinations across Uganda.
Are child seats available upon request?
Yes, child seats can be provided upon request to ensure the safety and comfort of young passengers.
Can I make last-minute bookings?
Yes, we accept last-minute bookings depending on vehicle availability. However, we recommend booking early to secure your preferred vehicle.
Do you offer tour and travel packages?
Yes, we offer customized tour packages for individuals, families, and groups, including wildlife safaris, city tours, and adventure trips.
Is fuel included in the rental cost?
Fuel is generally not included unless specified in your package. We provide clear pricing details before confirming your booking.
Do you allow cross-border travel?
Yes, cross-border travel can be arranged with prior notice and proper documentation. Additional charges and conditions may apply.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept mobile money, bank transfers, and cash payments for your convenience.
Do you offer luxury and VIP vehicles?
Yes, we provide a range of luxury and VIP vehicles suitable for special occasions, executive travel, and premium experiences.
Are your services available 24/7?
Yes, Fastlane Transport operates 24/7 to ensure you can access our services anytime you need them.
